About

Engineering education, mostly in Thai

I've been writing and teaching workshops for over a decade — Cisco labs back in the day, then Kubernetes, then container security, and now AI agents and the Anthropic ecosystem. The through-line is the same: take something complex, write it down clearly, in both the language the docs are in and the language my students think in.

Most of my recent work is bilingual — English for the technical primary sources, Thai for the explanations that actually unlock the concept for people in the room.
